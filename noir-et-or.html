The Philosophy

We do not add ornament. We remove everything until only the gold remains —
the rest is left to the dark.

Every Yaseen piece begins as a block of raw metal and a single sketch. No two stones are set the same way twice. What leaves the atelier is not a product, but a decision — about weight, about light, and about what a person chooses to carry with them.

In the Atelier

Four hands, one piece, six weeks.

Nothing at Yaseen is cast twice. From sketch to setting, every stage happens under one roof, at one bench, by the same pair of hands that will sign the piece.

01

Sketch & Wax

The design is carved first in wax, at true scale, before a gram of metal is committed.

02

Cast in Gold

18k gold is poured by hand and left to cool slowly, so the grain sets true.

03

Stone Setting

Each stone is seated and tightened under loupe, one prong at a time.

04

Hand Polish

A final polish by hand — the only step we refuse to automate.
